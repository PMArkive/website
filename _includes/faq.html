Frequently Asked Questions

Is fuckTimer going to be open source or private once completed?

Our goal is to provide a new completely new Timer, where anyone can contribute. So yes, it will be released as open source project.

Will fuckTimer support checkpoints and stages in bonus levels?

Yes.

When it's done? ETA? Release? Deadline?

It's done, when it's done. We do not have any deadlines for this project and you should know We are working here in your spare time - for free.
